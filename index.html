Spacing

The Button logo and mark should always be treated with care and consideration. There is a minimum area the logo and mark should be surrounded by.

The minimum clear-space of the logo and mark is equal to 30% of the width of the mark.

Usage

Our logo is the face of Button. Please adhere to the guidelines on how to appropriately use the logo.

DON’T place the logo on any unapproved colors, patterns, or textures.

DON’T make the logo transparent.

DON’T reorder the logo.

DON’T add any effects, filters, or shadows to the logo.

DON’T add or change colors of the logo.

DON’T rotate the logo.

DON’T skew or disproportionately stretch the logo.

DON’T add an gradients to the logo.
